Aliments you should eat to lose weight:

1/-Apples:

Apples are one of the most popular and healthy fruits for many good reason. Apples are high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, which are all beneficial to health.They also provide a variety of trusted antioxidants. These substances help neutralize free radicals and keeps you away from hunger. Free radicals are highly reactive molecules that can accumulate as a result of natural processes and environmental stresses. If too many of those accumulate in the body, they can cause oxidative stress, and this can lead to cell damage. This damage has been related to a number of diseases, including cancer and diabetes. So please consume with moderation.

2/-Pepper:

Peppers are an honest source of fiber, which is important for gut health. Including fiber-rich foods like peppers in your diet will help you fulfill the daily fiber recommendations of 1.1–1.2 ounces (30–35 grams) for men and 0.9–1.1 ounces (25–32 grams) for women. One cup (150 grams) of raw sweet pepper contains 0.08 ounces (2.5 grams) of fiber. Studies show that increasing your fiber intake can protect against gastrointestinal system conditions like carcinoma and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD). Eating fiber-rich foods also can help keep your gastrointestinal system functioning optimally by preventing constipation and promoting a healthy gut bacteria balance. Try snacking on sliced green bell pepper with hummus or adding chopped green bell pepper to salads, chili, and stir-fries for an additional boost of fiber.

3/-Cucumber:

Cucumbers are high in essential minerals as well as plant chemicals and antioxidants that can help treat and even prevent certain illnesses. Also, cucumbers are low in calories and contain an honest amount of water and soluble fiber, making them ideal for promoting hydration and aiding in weight loss.

4/-Salmon:

In addition to being an excellent source of lean protein, which may assist you maintain your muscle mass when trying to reduce , salmon is additionally full of healthy omega-3 fatty acids, which your body can’t produce. 100grams fillet of salmon has 206 calories, 22 grams of protein, 12 grams of fat, and 0 carbs. Salmon can be served as a supper with vegetables and a whole grain or starch like quinoa or sweet potato.

5/-Chicken breast:

Chicken breast is a great source of protein, which will help you like the salmon to maintain your muscle mass while losing weight.
Chicken breast contains 165 calories, 24 grams of protein, 1.5 grams of fat, and 2 grams of carbohydrates in a 100-gram meal. That means that protein accounts for about 80% of the calories in chicken breast, while fat accounts for 20%. Keep in mind that these amounts refer to a clear chicken breast with no added ingredients and that you should grill it because when you start frying it in oil or add marinades or sauces, the calories, carbohydrates, and fat content all go up.
